# PNG-DTF-SR-2021-001: BUSINESS RULES



-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
## SCHEME REGISTRATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
All participating entities must:
+ register with the scheme before displaying the YuTru brand on premises or on merchandise.
+ undertake annual internal reviews of internal systems, staff capabilities, and business processes against criteria set out in these rules.
+ be able to prove compliance with these rules.
